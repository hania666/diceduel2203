# Privacy Policy for Dice Duel

**Last updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction
Dice Duel ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile dice game built on Solana blockchain for Solana Seeker devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Wallet Address**
When you connect your Solana wallet, we store your public wallet address. We never access your private keys or seed phrase.

**Gameplay Data**
We collect gameplay statistics including: points balance, win/loss history, achievements, player level, and daily mission progress. This data is stored locally on your device and partially on our servers (Supabase) for the global leaderboard.

**Profile Data**
If you choose to set a nickname and avatar, this information is stored locally on your device.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To display your position on the global leaderboard
- To save your game progress between sessions
- To calculate daily missions and achievements
- To provide gameplay statistics

## 4. Data Storage
- **Local storage:** Game progress, profile, settings stored on your device via AsyncStorage
- **Cloud storage:** Leaderboard data (wallet address, points, game stats) stored on Supabase servers

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Leaderboard data (shortened wallet address and points) is publicly visible to all players.

## 6. Blockchain Data
Your public wallet address is visible on the Solana blockchain. We have no control over blockchain data.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Dice Duel is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 8. Points & In-App Currency
All points in Dice Duel are virtual and have no real monetary value. No real money transactions occur in the current version of the app.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
